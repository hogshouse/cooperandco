What Is Technical CRO?

Most Shopify stores are leaving significant revenue on the table, not because they lack traffic, but because that traffic isn't converting.

Technical Conversion Rate Optimisation (Technical CRO) is the practice of making precise, data-led changes directly inside your Shopify store to remove the friction that stops visitors from buying. It goes beyond advice and strategy... we actually implement the changes for you.

Fandango Digital already has deep insight into which of your pages are attracting traffic and where visitors are dropping off. Technical CRO is the natural next step: taking that intelligence and acting on it, month after month, to convert more of your existing visitors into paying customers.

No in-house developer needed. No separate agency to brief. Just measurable improvements, delivered alongside your existing SEO retainer.

01
SEO Data Identifies the Opportunity
Your existing SEO data shows us exactly which pages have traffic but aren't converting giving us a prioritised list of where to focus first.
02
We Audit & Plan the Changes
We analyse each page through a technical CRO lens, UX, page structure, speed, trust signals, calls-to-action etc and produce a concrete plan of improvements.
03
We Implement Directly in Shopify
We make every change live inside your store. Theme code, templates, apps, merchandising handled end to end without involving your team.
04
Track, Report & Repeat
Every change is tracked against revenue. We report monthly and feed results back into the next cycle, continuous improvement that compounds over time.
