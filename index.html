A Place In The Garden

A Place In The Garden is an award-winning producer of zinc planters, fountains, lighting and firepits.

When we were introduced to the family-led business they were not yet selling online, typical of this industry they were providing brochures at request and taking most orders directly over the telephone.

We built them a bespoke store using Shopify, our goal was for the site to be as beautiful and elegant as their print brochures had been, but infinitely more powerful!

One of the biggest pain points for the business was the amount of time spent by staff arranging deliveries. We implemented a custom system that automated the whole process freeing up 3 staff members, we also set up a POS (Point Of Sale) system for use at events and trade shows that allowed the APITG team to check stock, take deposits and arrange delivery times all in the presence of the customer while standing next to the product.

It's no wonder then that they won the 5 Star Trade Stand at the 2019 Royal Chelsea Flower Show! 🏅

Wingman Of The Road

Husband and wife team Kendal & Callie set out to revolutionise motorcycle touring with their product "The Goose", an All-In-One motorcycle camping system designed by bikers, for bikers.

Being a small team with a new product, our first build for them was an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) that was a single page site to allow them to sell asap and generate income for their fully-featured website.

This was very successful and a few months later we got the call that the targets had been met and it was time for us to build them a website to take them to the next level, Codename: "The Daddy".

What a journey so far, from filming the promo video on the south pacific highway in LA, to launching the new website to great fan-fare live at the Wheels & Waves motorcycle festival in Biarritz to being featured in GQ magazine, this has been an exhilarating ride and we have only just started!
